Tube Slides

Riders descend inside a large, inflatable tube that winds around the slide. Some tube slides are enclosed, while others are open-air.

Body Slides

Large slides designed for groups or pairs, where riders use a raft to descend down the slide. These slides are typically wide and feature sharp twists and turns.

Wave Slides

A steep, almost vertical slide that gives riders a sudden drop, often with a brief moment of freefall before hitting the water.

Aqua Coasters

These combine elements of roller coasters and water slides, where riders are propelled uphill with the help of water jets.
